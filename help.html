ROCK!      PAPER!      SCISSORS!

The game is has two levels: EASY and HARD.

Easy:
Rock ✊ smashes Scissors ✌️
Scissors ✌️ cuts Paper ✋
Paper ✋ wraps Rock ✊



Hard:
Scissors ✌️ decapitates Lizard 🦎
Lizard 🦎 stings Spock 🖖
Spock 🖖 vaporizes Rock ✊
Rock ✊ smashes Scissors ✌️
Scissors ✌️ cuts Paper ✋
Paper ✋ wraps Rock ✊
Lizard 🦎 eats Paper ✋
Paper ✋ disproves Spock 🖖
Spock 🖖 smashes Scissors ✌️
